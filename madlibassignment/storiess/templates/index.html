Welcome Choose a Madlib!

A Mad Lib is a word game created by Leonard Stern and Roger Price, where players fill in the blanks in a story with their chosen words. The game typically involves one player prompting others for a list of words, which are then inserted into a pre-written story, often resulting in humorous and nonsensical narratives. Mad Libs are commonly played as a party game or a fun pastime.
